Eclair Cake

This dessert is perfect for summer as it is cold and tasty!

Ingreditents

  • Milk
  • Whipped topping and pudding mix
  • Graham crackers
  • Chocolate frosting

Steps

  1. 1: Stir the milk, whipped topping and pudding mix together.
  2. 2: Line a baking pan with graham crackers.
  3. 3: Spread half of the whipped topping mixture on top of the graham crackers.
  4. 4: Repeat once, ending with a layer of graham crackers.
  5. 5: Chill the cake, cover with frosting, then chill again.
